Just Learn Organic Farming Academy:


Just Learn has established its Organic Farming Training Academy to provide consutancy services to make land organic and to grow organic produce with Nano certification courses at Raipur Khander Ranches, Opposite Amity University, Noida. The Training academy is built in an area of 24 bigha.

We are providing trainings to lacs of farmers and future farmers with nano certification courses on hybrid mode and also provide trainings on crop’s sales & marketing and entrepreneurship.

This will play a vital role to supply best crops and for economic growth of the country. Just Learn is also providing trainings on agriculture to the young generation to uplift the agriculture economy of the country.
