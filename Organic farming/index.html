Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy?

Just Learn has established its Organic Farming Training Academy to provide consutancy services to make land organic and to grow organic produce with Nano certification courses at Raipur Khander Ranches, Sec 126, Opposite Amity University, Noida. The Training academy is built in an area of 24 bigha. We are providing trainings to lacs of farmers and future farmers with nano certification courses on hybrid mode and also provide trainings on crop’s sales & marketing and entrepreneurship. This will play a vital role to supply best crops and for economic growth of the country. Just Learn is also providing trainings on agriculture to the young generation to uplift the agriculture economy of the country.

How long is the Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy program?

The Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy program is 10 days long and is offered in a hybrid format.

What will I learn from the Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy program?

The Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy program will provide you with a comprehensive education in organic farming, sales and marketing, and entrepreneurship.

What makes Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy the best choice for farmers?

Just Learn Organic Farming Training Academy stands out from the rest due to its few guest lectures in from IIM Lucknow faculty, its 10-day hybrid program, and its comprehensive focus on organic farming, sales and marketing, and entrepreneurship.
