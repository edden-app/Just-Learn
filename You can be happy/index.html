Frequently Asked Questions

What is You Can Be HappY?

You Can Be Happy is an online live course which teaches the learnings of Bhagavad Gita to help you find answers to life questions and excel in their professional careers.

Who will benefit from You Can Be HappY?

You Can Be Happy is designed for those who are seeking to explore the answers to life questions and learn how to balance their professional and personal lives. It is perfect for anyone who wants to gain an in-depth understanding of Bhagavad Gita and its teachings.

What will I learn with You Can Be HappY?

You Can Be Happy provides an in-depth understanding of Bhagavad Gita and its teachings. You will gain practical knowledge on how to find answers to life questions and apply it to your professional and personal life. You will also learn how to balance your professional and personal life.

How long does the You Can Be HappY course last?

The You Can Be Happy course is a 15 Sessions of 1-Hour Live Classes with content, with each session focusing on a different set of topics and also a package of Power of 3. Every session is filled with engaging activities, insightful lectures, and interactive discussions. By the end of the course, learners will have a comprehensive understanding of Bhagavad Gita and its teachings and finally to attain Attain ‘’Sukh, Shanti, Safalta & Suraksha’’
